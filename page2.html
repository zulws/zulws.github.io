Why are we disbanded?

12 member girl group, IZ*ONE announced their disbandment in April 2021, which is believed to be because of their first signed contract coming to an end.

When the announcement came out on March 10th, 2021, WIZ*ONEs trended a petition called '#IZONEPermanent' started to trend. They have been trending various hashtags related to a permanent IZ*ONE for over a year now.
